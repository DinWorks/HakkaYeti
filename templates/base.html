Hakka Restaurant

If you haven’t yet fallen in love with Hakka Chinese cuisine, you’re in for a treat! Located in London, Ontario on Clarke Road and Trafalgar Street, Hakka Yeti is one of the most sought-after family restaurants specializing in Hakka Chinese-inspired cuisine. We provide the fusion of Indian Chinese cuisine and aromatic Thai curries and fried rice. Hakka cuisine is the cooking style of the Hakka people, who originated in the southeastern Chinese provinces of Guangdong and Fijian. Our chef at the Hakka Yeti prepares authentic dishes that delight the taste buds. Our sauces are made fresh with the best available ingredients to create the perfect blend of flavor. The crunchiness of the garlic pepper chicken, fish cooked in a light delicious coating, tempered in spices that cool the palate, gently tossed noodles, glistening with a rich sauce, light fluffy fried rice, the piping hot soup- there’s something for everybody at Hakka Yeti!
